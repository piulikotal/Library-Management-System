Rules and Regulations

  • Silence must be observed in the Library
  • Loitering, gossiping, smoking, eating are strictly prohibited
  • Users should switch off their Mobile Phones inside the Library.
  • Users are advised to avoid talking and / or discussion so as not to create disturbance to other readers.
  • Members are required to carry their Library membership Card while visiting Library and must show them at the request of the Library Staff Members.
  • Users are requested to switch off electric fans and lights when not required.
  • Multilation of library materials and damage to library property are serious offences. The members shall be held responsible for any damage done to the documents or any other property belonging to the Library, and shall be required to replace such books / property damaged.
  • Students are required to enter into the Library with descent dress for availing of the Library facilities.
  • All members associated with Institute should obtain Clearance Certificate from the Library before being released from the Institute.
