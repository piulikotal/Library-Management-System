FAQs

Q: How do I search for books?

A: To search for books in our online library, follow these steps:
1. Visit our library website
2. Go to the search page
3. Enter the title, author, or keywords related to the book you are looking for
4. Click the search button
5. The search results will be displayed, showing available books matching your search criteria.

Q: How do I borrow books?

A: To borrow books from our online library, you need to:
1. Log in to your library account
2. Search for the book you want to borrow
3. Click on the book title to view details
4. If available, click the "Borrow" button
5. Confirm the borrowing process
6. The book will be added to your account, and you can check it out from your account page.
